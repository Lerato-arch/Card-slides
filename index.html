• Developer

    Software developers design,program,build,deploy and maintain software using many different skills and tools.

  • Designer

    People who plan the form or structure of something before it is made by preparing drawings or plans.

  • Marketer

    A professional who develops and implements strategies to promote and sell a company's products or services aiming to connect with target audience.

  • Editor

    A professional who refines written,visuals or multimedia content for publication or broadcast.

  • Gamer

    Someone who plays interactive games, either video games,tabletop role-playing games,skill-based card games or any other type of games.
